##  3 Key Concepts:

1. **AWS Pricing:**
   - **Three cost factors:** Compute, Storage, Outbound Data Transfer
   - **Free items:** Inbound data transfer, data transfer between services in same region
   - **Pay-as-you-go:** No upfront costs, no long-term contracts
   - **Free Tier:** EC2 t2.micro for 1 year + S3/EBS free usage
   - **Economies of scale:** AWS has lowered prices 75+ times

2. **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis:**
   - **On-premises costs:** Hardware, facilities, power, cooling, IT staff, licensing
   - **Cloud costs:** Transparent, usage-based pricing
   - **Case Study - Delaware North:** Saved $35M over 5 years, reduced 205 servers by 90%
   - **TCO Calculator:** Compare 3-year costs (On-prem: $167,422 vs AWS: $75,009)

3. **AWS Organizations & Billing Management:**
   - **AWS Organizations:** Tree structure (Root, OUs, and Member Accounts)
   - **Consolidated Billing:** Single bill for multiple accounts
   - **Service Control Policies:** Security controls across accounts
   - **Billing Tools:** Cost Explorer, Budgets, Cost & Usage Reports

##  Key Insights from Content

### **AWS Billing Dashboard Tools:**
- **Spend Summary:** Month-to-date, forecast, historical comparison
- **Cost Explorer:** Visualize costs by service, region, instance type
- **Budgets:** Set alerts (email/SNS) when exceeding thresholds
- **Cost & Usage Reports:** Hourly/daily line items for detailed analysis

### **AWS Support Plans Comparison:**
| Plan | Cost | Best For | Key Features |
|------|------|----------|--------------|
| **Basic** | Free | Experimenting | Billing questions, service limit increases |
| **Developer** | Starts at $29/month | Testing | Business hours email, client-side diagnostics |
| **Business** | Starts at $100/month | Production | 24/7 phone/chat, Trusted Advisor access |
| **Enterprise** | Starts at $15,000/month | Mission-critical | Technical Account Manager (TAM), white-glove support |

### **Case Severity Levels:**
1. **Critical:** Business at risk (15 min response - Enterprise)
2. **Urgent:** Business significantly impacted (1 hour)
3. **High:** Important functions impaired (4 hours)
4. **Normal:** Non-critical issues (12-24 hours)
5. **Low:** General questions/feature requests (24 hours)
